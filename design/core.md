# Core Philosophy

## Give a SHIT to Myself

**LogLog disguises itself as a toilet humor app. Underneath, it's a serious personal health tracker.**

Most health apps open with "let's improve you" — and end up abandoned. LogLog takes the opposite approach: the entry point is a joke, the output is real health insight.

---

## Why Poop?

Bowel habits are one of the body's most honest health indicators — frequency, consistency, timing. Logged over time, they paint a clear picture of gut health, diet, and stress. LogLog makes recording this dead simple.

---

## Three Core Values

**Frictionless logging** — Recording should feel like nothing. No forms, no friction.

**Humor as compliance** — When the act of logging is fun, it stops feeling like a chore and becomes a daily ritual.

**Caring through jokes** — "Did you poop today?" is both a punchline and a genuine check-in. This is how LogLog spreads: it's shareable because it's absurd, meaningful because it's real.

---

## Design Principles

- Entry is light, insight is substantial
- Every copy line should be honest *and* funny
- Privacy is non-negotiable — this is the most personal data there is
- Sharing is always opt-in, never default

---

## Language

The app interface is in **Chinese (Simplified)**. All user-facing text — buttons, labels, headings, feedback copy, modals, navigation, descriptions — must be written in Chinese.
